1 minute read

Ava ~ Blind devotion

Skilled with the spear and with a tongue as sharp as her blade, this headstrong, gossip-loving kitsune escaped life as a royal family relative back on Krakenspire, to the sweet comfort and freedom of the Dolphilie. She settled in Hye’s manor after meeting Lurleen, officially working as her bodyguard; but everybody can see the powerful sisterly love bonding these two. Things start going south as Vladimir shows up – the worst person to put in front of Ava – and it’s only a matter of time before the bomb goes off.

Tried new things – it went in a more graphic style this time. I like the effect on this piece, but I think I prefer the softer feel of the previous one. I don’t want to render my drawings because I don’t enjoy the process of painting, but it’s still nice to have a quick shaded effect, so I probably won’t make more pieces in this clean coloring style.

As always, adding text is super fun and adds so much to the whole thing. I really ought to sort my fonts tho, scrolling a list for 30 minutes trying out every single one is not scaleable at all x)
